# Projects

## Privacy & cryptography

- **[Shielded Protocol](https://github.com/Shielded-Protocol)** — a ZK privacy protocol on Stellar/Soroban that I lead. Spans Circom circuits (Groth16 withdraw proof, Poseidon hash, nullifier derivation), Rust/Soroban contracts (commitment pool, Groth16 verifier, compliance registry), a TypeScript SDK, a Next.js frontend, and documentation.
- **[Covert](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/Covert)** — confidential payroll on Ethereum using Zama's FHEVM. Salaries, bonuses, and peer recognition are processed fully encrypted on-chain, with per-employee decryption via EIP-712 signatures. Built for the Zama Season 3 Builder Track.
- **[ficho](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/ficho)** — sealed-bid cross-border FX matching powered by fully homomorphic encryption.
- **[Prediction-Market (StarkBet)](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/Prediction-Market)** — a privacy-preserving prediction market on Starknet. Bitcoin deposits via the Lightning Network are atomically swapped to STRK and shielded in a Pedersen commitment vault, so bets and positions stay unlinked on-chain.
- **[Shamir-Secret-Sharing](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/Shamir-Secret-Sharing)** / **[Merkle-Tree-Implementation](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/Merkle-Tree-Implementation)** — cryptographic primitives implemented from scratch in Rust.

## Payments & financial infrastructure

- **[OpenBridge](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/OpenBridge)** — a Rafiki-based Open Payments account provider issuing real wallet addresses to Nigerian, UK, and US accounts.
- **[bityield](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/bityield)** — a Bitcoin yield dashboard connecting passive BTC holders to live yield protocols on Stacks, with no prior DeFi knowledge required.
- **[Lepta](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/Lepta)** — pay-per-second livestream payments, provably delivered and settled in USDC on Arc.
- **[roampay](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/roampay)** — cross-border payment protocol on Solidity.

## Apps & systems

- **[Valor](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/Valor)** — a real-time 1v1 stat-duel shooter on GoodDollar + Celo, with identity verification, XP progression, and an on-chain gear marketplace.
- **[GemJar](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/GemJar)** — a match-3 puzzle game on Celo with a daily prize pool and an on-chain savings jar.

- **[banking-system](https://github.com/MarvyNwaokobia/banking-system)** — a command-line banking system in Rust with SQLite-backed persistence for accounts and transactions.
